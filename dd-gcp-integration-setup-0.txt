1. Access the Organization Policies Page:

In the Google Cloud Console, navigate to the Organization policies page:
Go to All products & services > Governance > Organization policies.

2. Select the "Domain Restricted Sharing" Constraint:

From the list of constraints, select Domain Restricted Sharing.

3. Manage the Policy:

Click Manage policy.
Under Applies to, select Override parent's policy.

4. Add a Rule:

Click Add a rule.
Under Policy values, select custom.
Under Policy type, select Allow.
Under Custom values, enter the following:
C0147pk0i (This is Datadog's customer identity)

5. Click Done:

Click Done to save the changes.
